Pizza Recipe

The Best Pizza is Homemade

The crust, sauce, and toppings of your homemade pizza are the bases of your pizza In the following recipe we will go through how to best make your pizza!

The crust should be crispy, and store bought dough can be used for your homemade pizza. You should make sure to give your pizza dough plenty of time to rise. Pre-made marinara sauces are great for homemade pizza, or homemade sauces in my opinion are even better! Finally be sure to use fresh vegitables for your toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 ounces low moisture mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup no salt added canned crushed tomatos
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 leaves fresh basin, torn
